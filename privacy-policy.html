Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 31, 2026

Jotters Tech Private Limited ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families who use our applications. This Privacy Policy explains how the Kids Calculator application ("App") handles information when you use our services.

About Kids Calculator

Kids Calculator is an educational application designed to help children learn and practice basic arithmetic skills, including addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. The app provides a safe, fun, and interactive learning environment for young users.

Information We Collect

Kids Calculator is designed with children's privacy in mind.

  • We do not collect personal information from children.
  • We do not require account registration.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location information.
  • We do not collect photos, contacts, or other personal content.

Educational Progress Data

To improve the learning experience, the app may store educational progress locally on the user's device, such as:

  • Game scores
  • Math practice progress
  • App settings and preferences

This information remains on the device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Advertising

Kids Calculator does not display third-party advertisements to children.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share personal information with third parties because we do not collect personal information through the app.

Children's Privacy

Protecting children's privacy is extremely important to us. Kids Calculator is designed for children and follows privacy-focused principles. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Security

We take reasonable measures to maintain the security of the application and protect any locally stored information from unauthorized access.

Third-Party Services

Kids Calculator does not use third-party services that collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

About Us

Jotters Tech Private Limited has been creating educational and child-friendly learning solutions since 2015. Our mission is to make learning enjoyable, engaging, and accessible for children through innovative educational tools and experiences.
